Controls:
Game
Esc: quit
Q: shake camera
Hero
W: move up
A: move left
S: move down
D: move right
F: pint location to console
Portal
Up: move up
Left: move left
Down: move down
Right: move right
Camera
H: focus on hero
L: focus on left minion
R: focus on right minion
P: focus on portal (zoom only)
N: zoom in center
M: zoom out center
J: zoom in focus
K: zoom out focus
